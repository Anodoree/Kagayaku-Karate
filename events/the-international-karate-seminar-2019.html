The International Karate Seminar 2019

Kagayaku karate held «The International Karate Seminar 2019» in Asia.

Kagayaku Karate held an international all-style karate workshop.

    The following sections of karate were affected:
  1. Traditional technique in Karate (kihon) and its use for self-defense in real life.
  2. Basic technique — analysis and explanation for high-level practitioners.
  3. Tips and tricks for the speedy mastering of skills.
  4. Kumite practice: Advanced footwork in sparring (kumite) and so on.

Thank you all for visiting! Untill next time!
