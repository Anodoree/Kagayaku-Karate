7 days Self-defense seminar for students in Asia. May 2019

The seminar was held for university students. The guys had absolutely no experience in the martial arts.

    The objectives of the seminar were the following sections:
  • what is self-defense;
  • what to do in a situation that requires an immediate response to impending aggression;
  • how to properly assess the degree of threat as such;
  • evaluation of their own forces when meeting with aggression on the street;
  • base knowledge and skills necessary to counter the threat;
  • self-defense for women;
  • simulation of common types of attacks;
  • ways of peaceful exit from situations with a life threat;
  • first-priority tasks in protecting themselves and their loved ones in a critical situation
    • and many many others.
